The 10-day curriculum

Ten days, each pairing a short lesson with a hands-on task and a daily LinkedIn habit. Work through them in order — every day builds toward launch. படிப்படியாக!

DAY 01Setup

LinkedIn Setup & Course Orientation

  • Welcome to the course
  • Why personal branding matters
  • Why LinkedIn is important
  • Creating a LinkedIn account
  • Choosing a profile photo
  • Basic profile setup
Today's Task
  • Create or optimize your LinkedIn profile.
  • Connect with 20 professionals.
  • Publish your first post: “Today I started my Personal Branding journey with CareerByteCode!”

DAY 02Positioning

Know Yourself & Find Your Niche

  • Self-discovery
  • Finding your strengths
  • Choosing your niche
  • Setting career goals
Today's Task

  • Publish a LinkedIn post about your career goals.

DAY 03Positioning

Build Your Personal Brand

  • Personal branding fundamentals
  • Defining your brand values
  • Writing a brand statement
  • Storytelling
Today's Task
  • Write your personal brand statement.
  • Share your personal story on LinkedIn.

DAY 04Optimize

LinkedIn Profile Optimization

  • Crafting your headline
  • Writing the About section
  • Experience
  • Skills
  • The Featured section
Today's Task
  • Complete your LinkedIn profile.
  • Publish one LinkedIn post.

DAY 05Optimize

Networking

  • Building connections
  • Sending connection requests
  • Commenting professionally
  • Growing your network
Today's Task
  • Connect with 30 professionals.
  • Comment on 10 posts.
  • Publish one LinkedIn post.
06
DAY 06Create

Canva & Content Creation

  • Canva basics
  • Personal branding design
  • Writing engaging posts
Today's Task
  • Create one Canva design.
  • Publish it on LinkedIn.

DAY 07Create

Blogging & Video Introduction

DAY 08Create

Today's Task

DAY 09Showcase

Portfolio & Online Presence

  • Portfolio basics
  • Resume basics
  • Building a professional online presence
Today's Task
  • Create a simple portfolio.

Personal Brand Launch

Today's Task
